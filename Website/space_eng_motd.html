Navigation

The Black Hole Edited mod causes you to stick to shipping lanes. If you go outside of these lanes you may die due to damage caused by the gravity of the black holes. Once you feel the gravity you have about 10km before your ship starts getting ripped apart.

!--WARNING--!

When navigating to the moons you must first fly to the correct earth gateway. Once there stop your ship and align with the moon gateway using GPS. After alignment is achieved launch from the earth gateway to the corresponding moon gateway.
DO NOT DEVIATE FROM THE PATH, DEVIATION = DEATH!
Use GPS Coordinates to find the gateways to the entrances of each shipping lane.

Rules

-1) No Griefing. The definition of Griefing is defined by the server admins and will be handled on a case by case basis. Use common sense. If you're here just to ruin everyones fun you will be banned.
-2) You must be in a faction. There is a faction called NEW you can join if you don't want to create your own.
-3) You must have an antenna or beacon on your ship or base set to 1KM or higher to prevent it from being raided while you are offline.
-4) No offline raiding. To raid a faction's base, simply check the faction tag on the antenna or beacon signal. Find out what the faction's full name is then hit F3 to check if someone from that faction is online. If someone from that faction is online you can raid their base or steal their ship. Basically, don't go near someones base if they're offline. Use common sense.
-5) No Tunneling player or NPC bases. (The act of attacking a base by digging under it then coming from below.)
-6) No building bases in protected shop space. It will be deleted. Also, bases within 5KM of a trade shop will not be restored if griefed. (The farther away from spawn you build your base the better.)
-7) We will put a safezone around player owned shops. You are not allowed to keep your personal items in the shop. If we find you using a playershop to keep your items safe we will delete said items with no warning.
